MENU

Sadya

Authentic Kerala's special sadhya which comes with sixteen dishes including two payasams.

Kizhi Porotta

Kerala porotta packed in banana leaf with curry roast & fry and steamed on a tawa to give the flavours of the meat.

Chicken Puttu

Traditional Kerala dish where a marinated whole chicken is stuffed with a spiced mixture of onions, eggs, and coconut, then slow-cooked inside a bamboo tub.

Biriyani

Biryani, in its most fundamental form, is a layered rice dish made with Indian spices, rice, fresh herbs, and usually some type of protein, such as meat, cheese, or egg.

Dosa

Dosa consisting of a thin, crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture. It is typically served with coconut chutney, sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew), and tangy tomato chutney. This savory delicacy is cherished for its flavorful filling and contrasting textures.

Alfam

Chicken marinated in yogurt, lemon juice, garlic, ginger, and other Middle Eastern spices. This delicious Arabic grilled chicken is best served with rice, salad, or khubz bread along with traditional sauces like garlic sauce & tahini.
